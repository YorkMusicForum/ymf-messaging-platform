YMF Messaging Platform – Admin Guide

As an admin (you or Sue), you can:
- Approve or remove members
- Add/remove users from chat rooms
- Send messages to all users in a room
- Upload large files (video scores, audio etc.)
- Limit member uploads to 10MB
- Access all messages and media posted
- Enforce quiet hours (9pm–8am)
- Turn notifications on/off for your account
